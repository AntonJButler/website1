The Dark Side

What was the Dark Side of the force?

The dark side of the Force, also known as Bogan or the shadow, was one of two ways of channeling the Force. To tap into the power of the dark side was to indulge in raw emotions such as passion, anger, and hatred.
The dark side was greed, the fear of change, and the inability to let go. By holding on to things, one became angry and hateful, which in turn led to suffering.
Those who were unable to conquer their dark side were devoured by it. The Sith were Force-sensitives well known for serving the will of the dark side and being the mortal enemies of the Jedi, who served the will of the light side of the Force.
