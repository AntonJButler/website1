The Light Side

What was the Light Side of the force?

The light side of the Force, also known as Ashla, was one of two methods of using the Force.
The light side was aligned with calmness, peace, and passiveness, and was used only for knowledge and defense. The Jedi were notable practitioners of the light, being selfless servants of the will of the Force

selflessness

The light side of the Force was selflessness. Its Jedi practitioners lived in the present, clearing their mind, letting go of their consciousness, and listening to the Force, acting on instinct.
The Jedi Order, which was devoted to the light side of the Force, had the core principle of being the guardians of peace and justice, which was exemplified by their altruism during the High Republic Era.
